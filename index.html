HOW TO PLAY FLIP 7

Goal

Collect as many unique numbered cards as possible without going over 7 different values. The player closest to 7 — or exactly 7 — wins the round.

On Your Turn

  • FLIP — Draw the top card from the deck.
  • STOP — Bank your current cards and wait safely.

Card Values (1–12)

  • Each value counts as 1 unique card.
  • Duplicates: drawing a number you already have causes a BUST — lose all cards.
  • If you hit exactly 7 unique numbers — FLIP 7! You score a 15-point bonus.

Special Cards

  • FREEZE — Skip the next player's turn.
  • +2 BONUS — Add 2 to your score immediately.
  • SECOND CHANCE — Ignore one duplicate (use once).

Scoring

  • Score = sum of your unique card values.
  • FLIP 7 exactly = +15 bonus points.
  • Bust = 0 points for the round.
  • First to reach 200 points wins the game!
